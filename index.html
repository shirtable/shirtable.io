Learn to program.

Python is not just a programming language. It's also a community that understands that code is more than just instructions for computers - it's for people, by people.

At Shirtable, we want to introduce you to a powerful programming language that runs on everything from the tiny Raspberry Pi all the way up to Google's Compute Engine. More than that, we want to help get you plugged in to a community of folks that want you to succeed and grow.

Shirtable hands-on workshops cover basic concepts and tools for calculation and visualization using Python. Participants are encouraged to help one another and to apply what they have learned to their own research problems.

We are Shirtable

Joshua R. Smith and Marty McGuire are Shirtable.

Joshua Smith's specialty is developing mathematical models of physical systems and implementing them in software using industry-standard software development practices. In his day job, Joshua studies energy conversion devices which convert either heat or radioactivity into useful electricity. Joshua has nearly a decade of experience writing Python code.

Marty McGuire is a web and operations developer with 12+ years experience building and deploying websites for clients such as MakerBot and Adafruit Industries.
